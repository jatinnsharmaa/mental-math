## Patterns to Remember

**Perfect squares ending in:**
- **0** → number ends in 0 (10, 20, 30…)
- **1** → number ends in 1 or 9
- **4** → number ends in 2 or 8
- **5** → number ends in 5
- **6** → number ends in 4 or 6
- **9** → number ends in 3 or 7

**Notable cubes:** 2³ = 8 · 3³ = 27 · 4³ = 64 · 5³ = 125 · 10³ = 1,000
